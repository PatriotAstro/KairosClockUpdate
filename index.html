KairosClock USB Flasher

This performs a full recovery-style USB update for KairosClock devices.

  1. Use Chrome or Edge (desktop).
  2. Connect KairosClock by USB data cable.
  3. Click Connect and choose the serial port.
  4. Install update and wait until completion.
Warning: this is a full-stack flash (bootloader, partitions, app, SPIFFS) and may clear saved settings on some devices. Uses conservative speed (115200) for reliability on older Macs/USB adapters.
